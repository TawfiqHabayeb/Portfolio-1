IClean

Iclean is a dedicated application for the teaching staff and the cleaning work in the university of jordan. It is a communication port between teachers and the cleaning crew, it allows the teachers to see which classrooms has been cleaned and the ones that requires to be cleaned, it also allows the teachers to request a cleaner to their office. The cleaners can see the the request that has been made by the teacher and whether to accept it or not.
